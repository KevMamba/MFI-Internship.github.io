## About the Program

Welcome to the **Monash Food Innovation Design Internship**.

This is a **12-week immersive program** designed to bridge the gap between creative concept and commercial reality.

Throughout the semester, you will gain hands-on experience navigating our cutting-edge innovation spaces while learning to bring a product from its initial spark to life.

By collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and key industry partners, you will develop a deep, practical understanding of the **FMCG landscape** and the strategic design thinking required to succeed within it.
